Carbohydrates VS Fat

Your body fuels endurance racing from two sources: stores of fat and carbohydrates. It's roughly half from each source (varies greatly depending on pace).

Why do Carbs matter?

Fueling from fat sources are limited during a race to what you already have, but carbs are different! You can continue to take in carbohydrate calorie sources during a race to increase your available energy. It's important to know how much you are burning so you can refuel yourself at the correct rate.
